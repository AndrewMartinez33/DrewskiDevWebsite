Someday, NASA will make me an honorary astronaut and send me off into space. If not, I know Elon Musk has my back. But here on Earth I am an aspiring web developer.

Throughout my academic career, I've thought about being an economist, an accountant, a photographer, etc. But none of them were any fun. There's something about sitting in front of a screen for hours, pulling my hair out, trying to figure out why my code won't run, and then realizing that it was just a syntax error, that makes web development fun! And I also love the problem solving, the creativity, and the never-ending opportunities to learn something new that comes with web development.

I graduated from California State University, Long Beach, earning a Bachelor's in Management Information Systems with Cum Laude Honor, in the fall of 2018. My journey in web development is just beginning and I am looking for a place to call home, where I can grow my skillset and contribute to the business.

I also spent a few years trying to become a photographer, which gave me some good experience with Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.
